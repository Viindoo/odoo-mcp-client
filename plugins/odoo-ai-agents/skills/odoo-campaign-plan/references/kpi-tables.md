# KPI sets by objective (odoo-campaign-plan Round 5)

Pick 2-3 leading + 2-3 lagging metrics for the campaign objective. For each, give a concrete
measurement method (Google Analytics event, LinkedIn Analytics export, email platform dashboard).

**Lead generation objective:**
- Leading: weekly new email list subscribers, LinkedIn post engagement rate, landing page unique visitors
- Lagging: demo requests submitted, qualified leads (MQL), cost per lead (if paid budget)

**Brand awareness objective:**
- Leading: content reach (impressions), share / save rate, blog session duration
- Lagging: branded search volume trend (Google Search Console), inbound contact form (unprompted), community mentions

**Release announcement objective:**
- Leading: announcement post reach, click-through to release notes / changelog, email open rate on launch day
- Lagging: trial sign-ups attributable to campaign period, upgrade rate (existing customers), press / partner mentions

**Retention / upsell objective:**
- Leading: email open rate (existing customer segment), webinar / demo registrations, feature adoption (if tracked)
- Lagging: NPS movement, upsell pipeline created, churn delta vs. baseline period
